Zulkifl Khan | Favourite Movies

Inception | Unbreakable | Interstellar

Welcome to my website, where I share my thoughts and opinions on three movies that I really like. These movies are Inception, Unbreakable, and Interstellar. They are all masterpieces of storytelling and filmmaking, and they have different themes and genres. Inception is a mind-bending thriller that explores the world of dreams and the power of the subconscious. Unbreakable is a realistic and psychological drama that examines the concept of superheroes and villains. Interstellar is a sci-fi epic that investigates the scientific and philosophical aspects of space travel and time dilation. These movies are not only entertaining, but also inspiring and thought-provoking. I hope you enjoy reading my reviews and analyses of them.
